Irish Cream Coffee Creamer

About this dish

This Irish cream coffee creamer, velvety and elegant, elevates your morning cup of Joe.

Ingredients

  • 1 quarter cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tsp of cocoa powder
  • 4 tsp of instant espresso
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp of kosher salt
  • 1 cup of half-and-half

Steps

  1. Heat heavy cream and sugar together in a small saucepan over medium heat
  2. Stir occasionally until sugar dissolves.
  3. Whisk in cocoa powder, espresso, vanilla, almond extract and salt.
  4. Remove from heat and cool slightly, then stir in half-and-half and refrigerate until ready to serve.
