French Onion Soup Rice

About this dish!

Transform an everyday side into a cheesy, show-stopping centerpiece. This recipe takes the beloved flavors of classic French Onion Soup and melds them into the most comforting baked rice dish youâ€™ll ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed French onion soup
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of uncooked long-grain white rice
  • 1 tbsp of sherry vinegar
  • 4 tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 3 quarter cups of shredded gruyere cheese
  • half a cup of pre-shredded low-moisture whole milk mozzarella cheese
  • fresh thyme leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220c.
  2. Place French onion soup, beef broth, rice, and sherry vinegar in oven and stir until evenly combined.
  3. Place sliced butter evenly over rice mixture, cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, remove and discard the foil, continue bake for 20-25 minutes until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  5. Increase temperature and broil with rack about 6 inches from heat source, sprinkle evenly with Gruyere and Mozzarella cheese.
  6. Broil until cheese is melted and golden brown and rice crisps up around edges of skillet for about 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves and serve immediately.
