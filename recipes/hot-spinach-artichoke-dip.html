Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

This spinach artichoke dip is delicious. It's so cheesy and fragrant. If you don't like artichokes, don't worry â€” you'll never know they're in there! My only question is: Is it okay to eat it with a spoon right out of the dish?

Ingredients

  • 1 block of cream cheese
  • 1 squeeze of mayonnaise
  • 1 bowl of grated parmesan, romano, and mozzarella cheese
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1 pinch of dried basil, garlic salt, salt, and pepper
  • 1 can of artichoke hearts
  • 1 thawed and drained spinach

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients, except the mozzarella, together.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a prepared baking dish, top it with cheese.
  3. Bake the spinach artichoke dip in the preheated oven until bubbly.
